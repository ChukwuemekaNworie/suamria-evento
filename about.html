About Us

Sumaria-Eventos is an event management company that is involved in events management and planning service of high quality at an affordable price for both outdoor and indoor event management service. We are committed to the provision of professional event planning service

Our company is founded to build upon an extensive array of expertise. The following is a list of events we plan;

  • Meetings, trainings, retreats
  • Conferences and workshops
  • Birthdays, anniversaries, graduations and holidays
  • Weddings , receptions and weekend getaways
  • Company picnics, banquets, and award ceremonies
  • Flight bookings, table reservations, customised brandings, and any other event that needs planning.
